Annihilation Game Trailer

This Trailer was made inside Cineam4D by myself as a way to market the game

The Trailer made use of X Particles plugin in Cineam4D. you may notice that there are a lot of people in order to cut down on rendertime I used GPU instancing

More intriguing information

The Shader was written by me in HLSL it was developed because as part of the game we wanted it to look like a borderland's art style

The Enemy and the player used Inverse Kinematics when aiming this was to try and make it look more realistic the enemy's IK was a bit hard to develop and took a lot of time since I had no concept on what IK was at the time.

The Airships flying in the sky was implemented with unity's particle system with GPU instancing turned on.
