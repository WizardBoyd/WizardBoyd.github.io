Story Behind the Game

This (work in progress) game is a piece for my one of my module's at Stafforshire University, it started off by me watching Twitch streamer's playing games like Jump King and myself actually liking that game. So I decided to make something simular.
However I wanted to try something different from what i had already worked with (aka Unity) so I decided to use a framework instead in this case it was the monogame framework.
